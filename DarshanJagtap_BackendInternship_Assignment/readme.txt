# Expense App
app that gives you a pie chart of your expenses and doughnut chart of your overall expense.

## how to run the app
"npm i" to install all the packages
"npm start" to start the app. the app will be serverd at localhost:3000.
include config.env with DATABASE and DATABASE_PASSWORD

## how to use the app 
click on the add expense button to add expenses. 
go to settings to add budget.
you can add categories from settings.
go to profile to change your email and name.
the app shows pie chart once there are expenses.
the app shows doughnut chart of overall expense once budget and expense have been entered.
